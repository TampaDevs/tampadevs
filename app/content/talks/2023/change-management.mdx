### Details

Join us for an enlightening session where we'll delve into the world of popular software development methodologies—Waterfall, Agile, Scrum, and Kanban. The core focus of our discussion will be on how these methodologies deal with change management in software projects, a critical aspect for all organizations to master.
By the end of this meetup, you'll walk away with a clear understanding of these methods and practical insights on how they can be deployed to bolster your development projects. Whether you're new to the field, looking to switch methodologies, or simply wish to refresh your knowledge, this event is for you!
We are thrilled to announce that our speaker for this event will be Mike Wood, a distinguished Technology & Engineering Executive and a passionate Agile Advocate.

**Speaker Bio:**

**Mike Wood**  
A results-oriented technology executive, Mike Wood brings a wealth of experience to the table. Known for his ability to provide innovative solutions and propel organizational success, Mike's leadership style blends technical knowledge, strategic vision, and exceptional leadership skills.  

Throughout his career, Mike has overseen investments of over $100 million across more than 100 technology projects and operational work streams. His adeptness in automating operations, software, and engineering has led to significant productivity improvements and cost savings for organizations. Don't miss out on this opportunity to learn from Mike's insights and experiences. Get ready to equip yourself with a deeper understanding of software development methodologies and how to adeptly navigate changes within your projects.
