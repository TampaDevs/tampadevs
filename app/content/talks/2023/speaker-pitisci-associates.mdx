### Details
Join us at the next Tampa Devs event, specifically tailored for the ambitious, tech-oriented job seekers among us. Tampa Devs, the fastest-growing nonprofit software development community in Tampa Bay, presents an engaging workshop presented with Pitisci & Associates focusing on interviews and job search strategies in the software industry.

**Description**
This event aims to empower participants with the skills and insights they need to secure their next role in the software development industry. Through interactive sessions, hands-on workshops, and informative talks, participants will gain an understanding of current job market trends, effective interview strategies, and valuable networking techniques.

**What to Expect:**

  1. Interview Skills Session: Learn the nuances of technical interviews from industry professionals. This session will include insights from industry recruitment professionals and tips on how to articulate your skills, projects, and experiences effectively.
  2. Resume and Portfolio Review: Get constructive feedback on your resume and portfolio from experienced professionals in the field. Learn how to showcase your skills and experience in the best light.
  3. Job Market Insights: Gain an understanding of what employers are currently looking for and how to stand out in the competitive job market.
  4. Networking Opportunity: Don’t miss this chance to meet and connect with like-minded individuals, industry experts, and potential employers. Bring your questions, ideas, and business cards!
