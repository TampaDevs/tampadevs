## Details
In DevOps, especially on the Ops side of the house, it seems you can’t outrun this Observability term, but what is it? Monitoring with a new name? A marketing gimmick for new infrastructure tools? And why should I care? And if I do care, how do I get started? This talk will introduce what Observability is, convince you to care, and give you some starting points and guidelines to think about as you get started.

We are excited to present our esteemed speaker for this event, Priscilla Parodi, whose extensive experience in user-centric roles brings a wealth of knowledge and insights.
