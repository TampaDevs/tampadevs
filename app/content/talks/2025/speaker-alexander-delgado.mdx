## Details
In this tech session, we will explore the fundamental distinctions between Relational Databases and Data Warehouses, shedding light on crucial concepts like row store and column store. We will delve into the physical storage of data on disk and examine how it influences performance.

Whether you're a seasoned professional or just starting your journey in the world of data, this session will provide valuable insights into the core principles that drive modern data storage and retrieval systems by helping you understand the contrasting characteristics of these two types of systems.
