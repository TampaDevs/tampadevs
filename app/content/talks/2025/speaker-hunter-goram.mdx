## Details
In the era of AI-driven development, it's never been easier to rush toward failure at unprecedented speeds. This talk reveals how teams unintentionally leverage powerful AI tools to accelerate mistakes, amplify technical debt, and crash projects spectacularly. More importantly, you'll discover how to shift your approach, turning AI from a runaway train into your team's greatest asset for boosting productivity and avoiding pitfalls.
