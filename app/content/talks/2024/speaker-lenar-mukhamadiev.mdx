## Details
Join us for an evening of hilarious horror stories and epic fails from the world of software testing with Lenar Mukhamadiev, the founder Idelsoft.com, a Tampa-based software consulting, who's seen it all. With 30+ projects delivered by Idelsoft, Lenar has witnessed firsthand the good, the bad, and the downright ugly side of software development.

Lenar will share the best testing practices and the mistakes to avoid. Learn how cutting corners on testing can lead to disastrous, sometimes hilarious outcomes, and why investing in a robust QA team isn't just smart—it's essential for your company's survival in the digital jungle. From the classic "it works on my machine" excuse, to not having QA processes, this event is a must-attend for anyone who's ever wondered what NOT to do when testing software.

So, bring your Jira bug reports and your testing stories for an evening. You'll learn, and you'll leave with a newfound appreciation for every QA engineer who's saved you from firing. This is a co-organized event with Tampa Bay QA & Testing.

## Speaker
**Lenar Mukhamadiev**
