## Details
Continuous Delivery extends the spirit of agility from development to production to keep pace with fast-changing business demands. To succeed in this environment, we must adopt several sustainable practices. 

![](/images/talks/2024/speaker-dr-venkat-subramaniam/highres_525033971.webp)

Without proper discipline and rigor, Continuous Delivery efforts can quickly turn into a nightmare for the teams and organizations that rely on them. In this presentation, we will discuss a dozen key practices, why each requires our focus, and how they impact our ability to maintain a steady delivery pace.
