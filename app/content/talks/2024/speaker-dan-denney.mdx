## Details
Get ready to dive into the dynamic world of Webflow, where cutting-edge low-code/no-code technology meets the finesse of development. This isn't just about simplifying web design for non-devs; it's about unlocking new potentials for seasoned developers like you, too.

Join us for an educational session where we'll learn the ins and outs of Webflow. You'll learn to manipulate templates, craft stunning layouts, and seamlessly export code - all while integrating AI tools to transform your vision into a viable website MVP in mere minutes.

And here's the best part: We're thrilled to host the renowned Dan Denney, a veteran in the realm of web development. Dan's insights and expertise promise to make this a great session.
