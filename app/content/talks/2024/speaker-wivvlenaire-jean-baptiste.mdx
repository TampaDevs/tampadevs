## Details
Have you ever had a unique idea for an app but didn't know where to start or how to execute it? You're not alone! Many aspiring creators have brilliant concepts but lack the roadmap to make their dreams a reality.

Join Wivvlenaire Jean-Baptiste , a Software Consultant and Engineer with 13 years of experience, for a fun, practical, and insightful discussion on building an app in "9ish" steps. This talk will guide you through a clear, actionable path from ideation to launch, providing alternative approaches along the way. Learn the secrets to turning your concept into a fully functioning app that stands out in today's competitive market!

Who Should Attend:

* Aspiring App Developers
* Entrepreneurs with Innovative App Ideas
* Product Managers, Software Engineers, and Marketing Professionals

Why Attend:

* Comprehensive Understanding: You'll walk away with a deeper understanding of the step-by-step process of building an app, backed by real-world examples and resources to guide you on your journey.
* Practical Insights: Enjoy practical insights and examples shared throughout the presentation, whether you're starting from scratch, refining an existing app, or simply looking for a second opinion.
* Actionable Strategies: Gain access to proven strategies that will help you succeed in developing a standout app.
