## Details
Join us for an engaging and informative workshop where you'll dive deep into the world of AWS, Terraform, and Kubernetes. Led by Cindy Singleton, this workshop is designed for those eager to learn about cloud infrastructure and automation.

**Key Lessons:**

* **Understanding AWS, Terraform, and Kubernetes:** Discover the basics of these powerful tools and how they integrate.
* **Deploying an EKS Cluster via Command Line:** Learn the why and how of deploying a Kubernetes cluster in AWS. Understand the importance of repeatability, quality, and speed in deployments.

**Hands-On Tasks:**

1. **Terraform Installation & Setup:** Install Terraform, and learn to configure `aws.tf` with your credentials.
2. **Step-by-Step Terraform Guide:** Write `.tf` files to deploy each component of an EKS cluster, including VPC, subnet, IAM roles & policies, EKS, Node Group, Nginx, and load balancers.
3. **Launching Your Website:** Deploy your website on EKS and access it online.
4. **Cleanup:** Conclude the workshop by safely destroying your cluster using `terraform destroy`.

This workshop is perfect for those looking to gain hands-on experience and a deeper understanding of cloud services and orchestration tools. Whether you're a beginner or have some familiarity with AWS and Kubernetes, you'll find this workshop both fun and enriching.
