## Details  
Join us for an engaging and insightful session as we dive into the challenges and triumphs of a real-world data migration for a mission-critical application. In this talk, we'll walk you through how we successfully planned, executed, and delivered a seamless migration that preserved data integrity for our client. This is not just a technical deep dive—it's a thrilling story. You’ll hear about the obstacles we faced, the strategic decisions we made, and the lessons learned along the way. Whether you’re a software engineer, CTO, or business owner, you’ll gain practical insights into how to plan for a successful migration, avoid common pitfalls, and mitigate risks.

By the end, you'll walk away with a clear understanding of how to approach your next data migration project with confidence, leveraging best practices and proven strategies that will save time and protect your most valuable assets—your data.
