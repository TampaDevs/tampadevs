## Details
Do you want to hear the story of how Amazon tuned Fire Channels, a popular FireTV app, to deliver a blazing fast experience for millions of devices? In this talk, you'll hear of how the Fire Channels team reduced app startup time by up to 70% in time for its release in 2022. You'll also learn skills about software performance that you can take back to your job. 

These skills include:
- Why mobile performance is important  
- How to measure Android performance  
- How to automate performance benchmarks in CI  
- Performance best practices
