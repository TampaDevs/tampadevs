## Details 

We celebrated the sucess of speaker Ryan Clements, a long time Tampa Devs member, speaker and founder of Byte Bot. Ryan shared with the Tampa Devs community, a story filled with success and failures, on how he and his team were able to delete $600K in software costs and the hard lessons learned along the way. By replacing an expensive third-party ETL platform, Ryan revealed the techanical challenges that required every ounce of the teams techanical know how to deliver.

This session dove deep into a story of a migration where the wins, the failures, and how the lessons were earned from being in the trenches. Attendees walked away with practical patterns for building resilient data pipelines and strategies for moving forward when requirements were fuzzy. But more importantly, a realistic picture of what it took to ship a high-stakes project without burning out the team!
