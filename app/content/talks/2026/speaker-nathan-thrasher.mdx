## Details  
Kubernetes deployments are difficult enough to the uinitiated—add in air gapped and classified environments, and the complexity skyrockets. When systems are entirely disconnected from the internet, teams must find creative ways that align with standard operating procedures (SOPs) to manage software delivery and updates.

![](/images/talks/2026/speaker-nathan-thrasher/1770123657218.webp)

Join us as Nathan Thrasher, a certified cloud native associate, helps break down the tools and workflows that make secure, repeatable Kubernetes deployments possible in isolated environments. Gain first-hand insights into solving the unique challenges of "dark site" software delivery.
